Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is made with cheese, lasagna sheets and lasagna sauce.

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Lasagna Sheets
  • Lasagna Sauce

Steps

  1. Warm up lasagna sheets
  2. Warm up and cook the lasagna sauce
  3. Layer the sauce and sheets
  4. Add cheese on top
  5. Cook in the oven until finished
